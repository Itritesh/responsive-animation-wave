ABOUT THOMAS

My Values

As your local representative, I am committed to amplifying the voices of our community. I believe in transparent governance, sustainable policies, and putting people before politics.

Every decision I make is guided by what's best for our community, not what's convenient for political parties or special interests.

MY PRINCIPLES

My Story

Born and raised in Kurrajong, I've spent my life deeply connected to our community. Before entering politics, I worked as a community organizer and small business advocate, giving me firsthand experience with the challenges we face.

When I'm not working for our community, you can find me volunteering at the local community garden, hiking our beautiful trails, or at the weekend farmers market with my family.

MY PRINCIPLES

Community First

Every decision I make starts with one question: "How does this benefit our community?" Politics should serve people, not the other way around.

Transparency

You deserve to know what your representative is doing and why. I commit to regular community forums and clear communication about my voting record.

Sustainability

Our policies must consider future generations. I advocate for environmentally responsible development and preservation of our natural spaces.

Inclusivity

Every voice in our community matters. I work to ensure all residents have access to services, opportunities, and representation.

Local Economy

Strong local businesses create jobs and strengthen our community. I support initiatives that help our small businesses thrive.

Independence

I am not bound by party platforms or special interests. I evaluate each issue on its merits and vote according to what benefits our community.
